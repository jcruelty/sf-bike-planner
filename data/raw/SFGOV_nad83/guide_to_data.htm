SFGIS Data Download

To download data, click hyperlink(s) to desired data below.
Decompression software, such as Winzip,
is required for decompression of data after download.
GIS software, such as ESRI or AutoDesk software,
is required to use vector data in the ESRI shapefile (.shp) format.
LizardTech software is required to use
image data in the LizardTech MrSID (.sid) format.
